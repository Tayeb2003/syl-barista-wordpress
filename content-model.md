# Content Model and Sitemap

## Sitemap

- Home
- Our Story
- Menu
- Experience
- Reservations

- Journal
- Contact

## Conversion goals by page

- Home: primary conversion to reservation, secondary conversion to menu
- Menu: drive in-store visit and pre-decision confidence
- Reservations: complete booking request with fast contact fallback
- Locations and Hours: map route starts and call actions
- Contact: direct enquiries and private event requests

## CTA hierarchy

1. Reserve Your Table
2. View Menu
3. Call Now
4. WhatsApp
5. Open Map

## Core content fields for admin

- Brand:
  - tagline
  - hero headline
  - hero subtext
- Business:
  - phone number
  - WhatsApp URL
  - reservation email
  - address
  - opening hours
  - map URL
- Menu:
  - category title
  - item name
  - tasting note
  - price
- Testimonials:
  - quote
  - guest name
- Story:
  - narrative intro
  - craft pillars

## Media checklist

- Hero photo (landscape, dark-friendly, 2000px+)
- 3 signature drink photos
- 2 process photos (barista action, latte art)
- 1 ambience wide shot
- Optional short background loop video (muted, <=8s)
